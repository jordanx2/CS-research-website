What is most interesting about Computer Science

One of the many interesting things about CS is how expansive the field is

It has a very technical, highly creative and imaginative component to the overall subject which leaves much room for a person’s artistic side to shine alongside their problem-solving nature

Any one individuals’ interest can be drawn in many different directions

The naturally fact that today’s society is so enriched with technology gives way to the emerging computer scientists and their knowledge that can have a profound effect on everyday life around the world

Computer science as a whole has the ability to equip bright and intelligent individuals with the tools to develop and deploy applications for millions of people around the global to interact with
