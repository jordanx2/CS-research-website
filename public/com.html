Introduction

Communications skills are vital throughout the whole fields of Computer Science. Properly conveying ideas and thoughts in a coherent fashion to team members and managers alike is of the upmost importance in any development situation. Problems can indefinitely be solved quicker if proper communication skills are used to communicate the nature of problems more efficiently. Communication skills in a development situation can be divided up into two categories.

Team Communications

Personal Communications

Being Open & Understanding

Understanding Role Within Team

Don't Be Too Aggressive or Too Passive

Enthusiasm Towards Your Ideas & Others

Active Listening Skills

Being Confident in Delivering Points Across

Open Body Commuication

Excellent Overall Written and Verbal Skills

When should communication be best adhered to?

At every stage along the Software development lifecycle

Software Development Lifecycle: When entering a workplace relating to a computer science role more often than not a structured process is typically followed at all times. In a software development role the software development lifecycle is what is used to develop software.

Plan

Design

Develop

Test

Deploy

Typically most of the SDLC models present today follow some kind of adaptation of this structure. A somewhat intuitive way to developing software. Some methodologies follow strictly to the stages previously mentioned. While others take a more iterative approach.

Traditional Models

Agile Models

Software development as a whole is taken as an example how best communication should be displayed whenever actively participating in a technical role within the field of computer science.

The SDLC is taken as an example from software engineering to show how important communication is at every stage within a project.

If communication is misinterpreted or not properly expressed early in the lifecycle of the project then the probability of impediments can increased drastically.

This is why clear and honest communication are some of the key virtues that are greatly appreciated within any given work dynamic. Any future impediments that can be anticipated ahead of times will allow for better clarity when moving forward with development.
